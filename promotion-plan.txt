Client: Acrylics Artistry

What product/service is being promoted?
Custom nail designs and nail art services.

Who is the target audience?
Those with a love for art and especially nail art. Those local to the area or nearby. Those
with a love for daring fashion, styles and desire to live as authentically as possible.

Which platforms are most suitable (Instagram, Youtube etc)?
Instagram, TikTok, Youtube and Twitter. Instagram and Twitter are great to showcase the finished nails and the custom designs that customers request. Whereas TikTok would be perfect for short clips of the session, which could then lead to the Youtube channel where the entire session could be viewed.

Proposed hashtags?
#nailart, #acrylics, #nails, #custom, #fashion,#beauty

Promotional strategy (2-3 sentences)

- Post nail art designs on both Instagram and Twitter while also ensuring a regular posting scheduel. Engage with those that follow the account and art trends that circlulate in the community (for example, when 'Sailor Moon redraw trend' was a thing, create sailor moon nails.)
-Regularly post short clips on TikTok and Youtube shorts, ensure that viewers are aware that the full custom nail design creation and nail art session will be up on the Acrylics Artistry Youtube channel.
